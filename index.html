BLACK HOLES TRIVIA 

I'm a fan of black holes, therefore 
I use the name Event Horizon as my brand.

Black holes are probably the most confusing 
and fascinating objects in the universe.
First predicted by Schwarzschild in 
his solution to General Theory of 
Relativity.

They are defined as objects with 
gravity pull so strong not 
even light can escape.

The point of no return is 
considered an imaginary 
boundary called the 
EVENT HORIZON.
